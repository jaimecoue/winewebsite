  • Black & White



    B

    Victor Eide , a designer from Sweden, created a new packaging for a line of premium wines. He used an approach that is never losing: a combination of white and black invariably creates an atmosphere of refinement and sophistication.

    According to the designer himself, he wanted to make a design that radically differentiate the brand among competitors using traditional design options. To implement his idea, he decided to use a graphic image of the bunches of grapes - white on black, black on white.



  • We Inspire



    W

    Charles le Chat is a range of premium wines for those inspired individuals who see the spark of possibility in everything around them.

    Produced by a team of dynamic wine creatives, this urban wine brand celebrates colourful thinking, doing, seeing, tasting, and living.

    ​ Wine should be an extension of your style. Keep up with your ever-evolving world. And if you are one of those people who likes to strum to the rhythm of their own sound and live out loud, then our wine is for you.
